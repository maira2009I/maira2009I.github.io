Introduction

Cooking any type of food, whether it be a sweet treat or something salty, spicy and savory all meals require hard work to produce. Many people enjoy cooking for a variety of reasons. It allows one to open up their creative outlets and make something that they love and will also bring satisfaction to others. Making good meals is also a way to connect with friends and family members.
Not only this but making any meal is a way people can learn new skills, and develop their own unique recipes which people love. Today I will be sharing with you some of my favourite meals that I enjoy making and eating. The different recipes that I will be talking about are listed below.
  1. Banana Bread
  2. Pasta

Banana Bread

Banana bread is a sweet, and easy treat to make.Banana bread has also become a popular way to use overripe bananas. In addition to this, many individuals have began to add their own twist to banana bread by adding diffrent ingredients such as nuts, chocolate chips,dried fruits, or other spices. Did you know that banana bread originated from the great depression. When times were hard everyone used whatever they could to survive. Instead of throwing away overripe bananas people utilized it in creative ways.

Pasta

Pasta is a type of savory cuisine which is formed from dough, which is then precisely cut into various diffrent shapes and sizes to create an outstanding dish. Pasta in itself is rather plain, but what makes this dish truly stand out from any other is its sauce which can be diffrent depending on everyones tastes. Even while pasta is commonly associated with the Italians did you know that pasta's origins date back to China?

Chocolate Chip Cookies

A chocolate chip cookie is a sweet baked treat that is known to have a buttery and crumbly texture on the outside, while having a creamy and gooey inside. The fascinating thing about cookies is the fact that there is never one type. Individuals have been able to make different variations of cookies to match every single person's taste. For example, some cookies include nuts, while others have oatmeal. Did you know that people in the US purchase over 2 million cookies per year?
